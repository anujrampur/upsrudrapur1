Welcome to UPS Rudrapur

We are thrilled to welcome you to our school community—a place where learning, growth, and friendships come to life. Whether you're a new student, a parent, or a staff member, we are so glad to have you here and can’t wait to embark on this journey together.

Our school is more than just a place for education; it’s a home for dreamers, innovators, and achievers. Here, we strive to create a nurturing environment where every individual feels valued, supported, and inspired to reach their fullest potential.

From the moment you step through our doors, you become a part of our family. Together, we will celebrate milestones, overcome challenges, and create lasting memories. Every voice matters here, and every contribution makes us stronger.

We believe in building not just bright minds, but compassionate hearts and confident leaders. Whether in the classroom, on the field, or in the arts, we aim to provide opportunities that help you discover your passions and develop lifelong skills.

Welcome to a place where possibilities are endless and your success is our mission. We can’t wait to see all the amazing things you’ll accomplish here. Let’s make this journey unforgettable—together!

About Us

At UPS Rudrapur,we strive to nurture student's potential and foster a love for learning. UPS Rudrapur is being operated under the aegis of Basic Shiksha Parishad Uttar Pradesh. Being a government school,the school is providing quality education to most deprived students. Mr. Himanshu Kumar Tiwari is acting as an incharge head of the school and his twenty plus years of experience is benefiting to the school as well as our students.
